JunctionFlow - Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-20

JunctionFlow ("the App") values your privacy. The App is primarily an offline single-player game experience and does not require account registration or sign-in.

1. Information We Collect

  • The App does not collect personal identity information such as your name, phone number, email address, or contacts.
  • The App does not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs and does not perform cross-app tracking.

2. How We Use Data

  • The App stores your game progress and settings locally on your device, such as level completion rate and sound or haptics preferences. This data is used only to restore and display your game experience.
  • Local data stays on your device and is not uploaded to our servers.

3. Permissions and Access

  • The App does not request access to location, camera, microphone, contacts, or similar permissions.
  • You can clear local progress through "Settings > Data > Reset Level Progress" in the app.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for general audiences and is not designed to collect personal information from children.
